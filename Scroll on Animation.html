Caramel Macchiato

Caramel Macchiato

Caramel Macchiato is a popular espresso-based drink that is typically made with steamed milk, espresso, vanilla syrup, and caramel sauce. The drink is served hot, and sometimes it's also available as an iced drink.

Chocolate Frappuccino

Chocolate Frappuccino

A chocolate Frappuccino is a type of blended coffee drink that's typically made with ice, coffee, milk, and chocolate syrup or powder. It's often topped with whipped cream and more chocolate syrup or shavings.

White Chocolate Mocha

White Chocolate Mocha

A white chocolate mocha is a sweet and creamy coffee drink made with espresso, white chocolate syrup, milk, and whipped cream. It's a popular variation of the classic mocha, which is typically made with dark chocolate.

Vanilla Frappuccino

Vanilla Frappuccino

A vanilla Frappuccino is a popular blended coffee drink made with ice, milk, coffee, and vanilla syrup. Like other Frappuccino flavors, it's typically topped with whipped cream and additional syrup drizzle.

Frappe

Frappe

Frappe is a type of cold blended coffee drink that originated in Greece, but has become popular in many other parts of the world. It's typically made with instant coffee, sugar, water, and ice, and blended until frothy.

Flat White

Flat White

A flat white is a coffee drink that originated in Australia and New Zealand, but has gained popularity in many other parts of the world. It's similar to a latte, but with less milk and a stronger espresso flavor.
